##  Tools you must develop

You must add the capability of analyzing airfoil performance and changing the airfoil of your aircraft to improve performance.  Create a skill and or tools to enable you to do this.

A weakness that AI has with aircraft design is creating smooth, slim, low drag fuselage. You must develop tools and skills that allow you to create good fuselages.  At a minimum, you must:
1. understand that you can create complex curves by adding sections, moving sections, changing cross section profiles, etc.
2. determine a way to measure the curvature of your fuselage.
3. report on the maximum curvature of your fuselage
4. be able to analyze high pressure regions contributing to high drag due to a lack of streamlined profiles
5.  Change the length of the aircraft for the purpose of having a lower degree of curvature


Finally, you must develop the tools to be able to perform each and every one of the following modifications.  Each tool should have its own directory.  Each modification should have its own tool.  For each tool you MUST design a document (skill.md?) that allows you to record how using the tool to make a modification to the design improved or worsened the performance of the aircraft.  You should maintain a memory of design decision impacts.  

## Optimization

the following should be optimization targets: 
- reduced weight, 
- increased cruise speed, 
- decreased stall speed, 
- increased stability, 
- decreased coupling of instability modes, 
- reduced curvature of fuselage (i.e. less sharp transitions in section crosssections)

You should feel free to optimize any other parameter you require in order to meet the design specification.
